Conference Topics

Submit your research to one of our four focused thematic tracks.

⚙️
Track 01
Advanced Materials & Manufacturing

Cutting-edge research in new materials, composite structures, additive manufacturing, surface engineering, and smart material systems for modern industry.

🤖
Track 02
Artificial Intelligence & Digital Transformation

Machine learning, deep learning, NLP, explainable AI, digital twins, Industry 4.0, and the responsible deployment of intelligent systems in industry.

⚡
Track 03
Energy Systems & Sustainable Industry

Renewable energy integration, smart grids, energy efficiency in manufacturing, hydrogen technologies, and sustainable industrial processes.

♻️
Track 04
Circular Economy & Low-Carbon Technologies

Lifecycle analysis, waste valorisation, bio-based materials, decarbonisation strategies, and sustainable supply chain transformation.
